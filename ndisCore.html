NDIS Core Supports

Disability Services 24/7 is dedicated to providing comprehensive and personalized support through the National Disability Insurance Scheme (NDIS) Core Supports. Our services are designed to assist individuals with disabilities in managing their daily life, participating in the community, and achieving their personal goals. We understand the importance of these foundational supports in enabling a life of independence and fulfillment.

NDIS Core Supports are the building blocks for a better quality of life. At Disability Services 24/7, we offer a range of Core Support services that are flexible and tailored to meet the unique needs of each individual we serve.

Our Core Support Services Include:

  • Assistance with Daily Life: To support individuals in living independently, we provide assistance with daily tasks such as personal care, meal preparation, and household chores.
  • Transport to Enable Participation: Recognizing the importance of community participation, we offer support with transportation to help individuals access community activities, work, and educational opportunities.
  • Social and Community Participation: We facilitate engagement in social and community activities, supporting individuals to build relationships, learn new skills, and enhance their social well-being.
  • Consumables: We assist in accessing and managing consumable products necessary for daily living, such as continence aids and nutritional supplements, ensuring individuals have the resources they need to manage their health and well-being.

Begin Your Journey with Disability Services 24/7

Choosing Disability Services 24/7 for your NDIS Core Supports means choosing a partner who is committed to your success and well-being. We are here to support you in navigating your NDIS plan and ensuring you have access to the essential supports needed to live a fulfilling life. Contact us today to learn more about our NDIS Core Supports and how we can assist you or your loved one in achieving independence and community participation.

At Disability Services 24/7, we are dedicated to facilitating access to the NDIS for eligible individuals, offering a spectrum of services tailored to meet the unique needs of each person we serve.
