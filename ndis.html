NDIS

What is NDIS

The National Disability Insurance Scheme (NDIS) represents a revolutionary approach to supporting Australians with significant and permanent disabilities. It's a comprehensive initiative aimed at providing personalized support to individuals and their families, ensuring a better quality of life and fostering community inclusion.

Care28: Empowering Lives Through NDIS Support

At Care28, we are dedicated to facilitating access to the NDIS for eligible individuals, offering a spectrum of services tailored to meet the unique needs of each person we serve. Our mission aligns with the NDIS's core aim: to provide support that empowers individuals with disabilities to live fulfilling lives, enriching both their own experiences and those of the community around them.

What Sets Us Apart

Care28 stands at the forefront of service provision within the NDIS framework, thanks to our:

  • Personalized Approach: We understand that no two journeys are the same. Our services are designed to be as unique as the individuals we support, ensuring that every NDIS participant can achieve their goals.
  • Comprehensive Support Services: From facilitating core supports for daily living to capacity-building initiatives that enhance skills and independence, Care28 covers the full spectrum of needs.
  • Commitment to Community:Beyond individual support, we embrace the Information, Linkages, and Capacity Building (ILC) component of the NDIS. Our goal is to foster inclusive communities where everyone can participate and thrive.

Who Is Eligible?

Eligibility for the NDIS through Care28 involves:

  • Age: Applicants must be under 65 years of age.
  • Residency: Australian citizens, permanent residents, or New Zealand citizens with a Protected Special Category Visa are eligible.
  • Disability Requirement: Individuals must have a permanent and significant disability that impacts their ability to participate in everyday activities.

Looking Forward

As we move forward, Care28 remains committed to enhancing the lives of individuals with disabilities through our specialized services and support. Our vision is not just to meet the immediate needs of those we serve but to contribute to a society where every individual has the opportunity to lead a life full of potential and participation.

Together with the NDIS, we are creating pathways to independence, opportunity, and community engagement for hundreds of thousands across Australia.
