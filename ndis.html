What is NDIS

The National Disability Insurance Scheme (NDIS) represents a revolutionary approach to supporting Australians with significant and permanent disabilities. It's a comprehensive initiative aimed at providing personalized support to individuals and their families, ensuring a better quality of life and fostering community inclusion.

Disability Services 24/7: Empowering Lives Through NDIS Support

At Disability Services 24/7, we are dedicated to facilitating access to the NDIS for eligible individuals, offering a spectrum of services tailored to meet the unique needs of each person we serve. Our mission aligns with the NDIS's core aim: to provide support that empowers individuals with disabilities to live fulfilling lives, enriching both their own experiences and those of the community around them.

What Sets Us Apart

Disability Services 24/7 stands at the forefront of service provision within the NDIS framework, thanks to our:

  • Personalized Approach: We understand that no two journeys are the same. Our services are designed to be as unique as the individuals we support, ensuring that every NDIS participant can achieve their goals.
  • Comprehensive Support Services: From facilitating core supports for daily living to capacity-building initiatives that enhance skills and independence, Disability Services 24/7 covers the full spectrum of needs.
  • Commitment to Community:Beyond individual support, we embrace the Information, Linkages, and Capacity Building (ILC) component of the NDIS. Our goal is to foster inclusive communities where everyone can participate and thrive.

Who Is Eligible?

Eligibility for the NDIS through Disability Services 24/7 involves:

  • Age: Applicants must be under 65 years of age.
  • Residency: Australian citizens, permanent residents, or New Zealand citizens with a Protected Special Category Visa are eligible.
  • Disability Requirement: Individuals must have a permanent and significant disability that impacts their ability to participate in everyday activities.
