Supported Independent Living (SIL) Services

Disability Services 24/7's Supported Independent Living (SIL) services are at the heart of our commitment to enabling individuals with disabilities to lead independent, fulfilling lives within their communities. Our SIL services are designed to support those who require daily assistance due to a significant disability, providing them with the necessary help to live as independently as possible.

Features of Our SIL Services

  • Customized Living Arrangements: We provide a variety of living arrangements that cater to the preferences and needs of our clients, from shared housing options to individual accommodations, all equipped to ensure safety, comfort, and accessibility.
  • 24/7 Support: Understanding the varying needs of our clients, we offer round-the-clock support for daily tasks, personal care, and emergency response, ensuring peace of mind for both our clients and their families.
  • Skill Development: Our programs include training and support in life skills such as cooking, budgeting, and household maintenance, empowering our clients to manage their daily lives more effectively.
  • Community Inclusion: We believe in the importance of social and community participation. Our SIL services facilitate access to community activities, education, and employment opportunities, enhancing the social well-being of our clients.
  • Health and Wellbeing: Our support extends to managing health care appointments, medication management, and coordinating with healthcare providers to ensure the overall health and wellbeing of our clients.

Begin Your Journey with Supported Independent Living

Choosing Disability Services 24/7 for Supported Independent Living means choosing a partner committed to your independence and quality of life. Our dedicated team is here to support you in achieving your personal goals and living the life you choose. Contact us today to learn more about our Supported Independent Living services and how we can support you or your loved one in leading a more independent and fulfilling life.

At Disability Services 24/7, we are dedicated to facilitating access to the NDIS for eligible individuals, offering a spectrum of services tailored to meet the unique needs of each person we serve.
