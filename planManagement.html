Plan Management

Care28 offers comprehensive Plan Management services to individuals navigating the complexities of the National Disability Insurance Scheme (NDIS). Our aim is to empower you with the freedom to focus on achieving your goals, while we manage the financial and administrative aspects of your NDIS plan. With Care28, you gain a partner who ensures that your funding is utilized optimally, giving you peace of mind and the ability to concentrate on what matters most—your personal growth and well-being.

Streamlining Your NDIS Experience with Expert Plan Management

Our Plan Management team brings expertise, transparency, and personalized support to every aspect of managing your NDIS plan. We are dedicated to making the process as smooth and efficient as possible, allowing you to enjoy the benefits of your plan without the stress of managing it.

Our Plan Management Services Include:

  • Financial Administrative Services: We handle all the financial operations associated with your NDIS plan, including processing invoices, making payments to providers, and tracking your budget. Our meticulous financial management ensures your funds are managed accurately and efficiently.
  • Budget Tracking and Reporting: Stay informed about how your funds are being used with our detailed budget tracking and regular reporting. We provide clear, understandable reports that help you make informed decisions about your services.
  • Choice and Control: With Care28, you maintain complete control over your choice of service providers. Our Plan Management services give you the flexibility to choose providers that best meet your needs, without the restrictions of being agency-managed.
  • Expert Advice and Support: Navigating the NDIS can be complex. Our team offers expert advice and support, helping you understand the intricacies of your plan and how to best use your funds to achieve your goals.
  • Seamless Coordination: We work closely with you, your family, and your service providers to ensure seamless coordination of services. Our proactive approach helps to avoid any disruptions in support, ensuring a smooth and effective delivery of services.

Embark on Your Journey with Confidence

With Care28's Plan Management services, you can embark on your NDIS journey with confidence, knowing that the financial and administrative aspects of your plan are in expert hands. Our goal is to provide you with the support and freedom you need to focus on achieving your personal objectives. Contact us today to learn more about how our Plan Management services can simplify your NDIS experience and support you in living your best life.

At Care28, we are dedicated to facilitating access to the NDIS for eligible individuals, offering a spectrum of services tailored to meet the unique needs of each person we serve.
