Disability Services 24/7 values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This document outlines the reasons why we collect, use, and disclose your personal information, and seeks your consent for these actions.

Handling and Disclosure of Your Personal Information

The personal data we handle may include details about you and any disabilities. This encompasses records made by us or other healthcare professionals, as well as images or videos capturing your conditions, taken by either us or other professionals.

Purpose of Using Your Personal Information

We utilize your personal data to deliver, manage, and oversee your care and any related activities.

Your personal information may be shared with:

1. Funding bodies associated with Disability Services 24/7

2. Your physician and other NDIS service providers

3. Government and regulatory entities, including the National Disability Insurance Agency, Medicare, state disability agencies, and the Australian Taxation Office

4. NDIS accredited auditors for audit and quality assurance purposes, relevant to our NDIS provider registration. You are automatically included in these audits but can opt out anytime.

5. Your representatives, including legal guardians, executors, trustees, and legal advisors

6. Law enforcement, the Disability Services Commissioner, or in response to legal mandates

7. Payment processing financial institutions

8. Contracted service providers of Disability Services 24/7

9. Other parties when required by law without your consent

10. Additional parties as mentioned in our Privacy and Dignity Policy

In certain situations, your data might be shared without your consent if the law mandates it.

Privacy Complaints and Incidents

Our Privacy and Dignity Policy details how you can access your data, lodge privacy complaints, and how such complaints are processed in line with our Feedback and Complaints Management Policy. We take privacy breaches seriously, managing them via our Incident Management System as described in our Incident Management and Reporting Policy. More information can be found within this policy.
