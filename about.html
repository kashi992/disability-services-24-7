Get to Know Us

Your Dedicated NDIS Partner

Disability Services 24/7 is here to support you in living a healthier, more independent life. As a compassionate and experienced NDIS provider, we tailor every service to suit your unique goals and lifestyle. Whether you need daily personal care, mental health support, or help navigating your NDIS plan, our friendly team is ready to walk beside you — every step of the way. We believe in accessible, respectful, and person-centred care that empowers you to live life on your terms.

Disability Services 24/7 — Where Care Meets Possibility

Helping You Live Your Best Life, Step by Step

Our goal is clear and meaningful: to help and support our clients to live their happiest and most fulfilling lives. We do more than just provide services; we create special plans that are just right for each person's own needs. Whether it's helping with daily tasks, offering therapy, or organizing support, our wide variety of services aims to make a lasting and good difference in the lives of those we work with.

Embodying the Australian Spirit

At Disability Services 24/7, our dedication to our clients is deeply inspired by the Australian ethos. We're intimately familiar with the local scene, our rich culture, and the specific challenges our community members navigate. Being 100% Australian-based is more than a detail; it's our vow to deliver support that vibrates with the true Australian spirit.

Together, Building Stronger Communities

At Disability Services 24/7, we're all about working together. Our support goes beyond the basics—we create a place where everyone's input matters, and every voice gets listened to. We're committed to shaping a community where everyone, no matter their background, can feel supported and part of something bigger

Driven by Passion, United in Support

At Disability Services 24/7, it's our team's true passion that makes us stand out. For us, this isn't just work—it's our mission. Our devoted team members do more than what's expected, fostering deep connections with clients through trust and understanding. We're dedicated to crafting an environment where everyone doesn't just receive support; they feel appreciated, valued, and listened to.

Heartfelt Care, Open Arms, Strong Voices

What really defines Disability Services 24/7 are our core beliefs. Empathy is our compass; it shapes how we connect with each client, offering sincere care and understanding. We're all about including everyone and speaking up for what each person needs. Our support is built on a strong base of respect, teamwork, and lifting others up, showing just how much we care.

Step Forward With Us: A Journey Towards Empowerment

Disability Services 24/7 welcomes you to be part of our empowering journey. Whether you need support or want to contribute to positive change, we value the strength found in coming together. Let's unite to inspire progress, champion inclusivity, and cultivate a community where everyone can flourish.
