Community Support Services

At Care28, we believe in the power of community to uplift, support, and empower individuals with disabilities. Our Community Support services are designed to foster inclusion, independence, and active participation in community life. By connecting individuals with a wide range of community activities and resources, we aim to enhance their social, recreational, and educational experiences.

Building Connections, Enhancing Lives

Our Community Support services offer comprehensive support that goes beyond mere participation, focusing on meaningful engagement that enriches the lives of our clients and their communities.

Key Features of Our Community Support Services

  • Social and Recreational Activities: We facilitate access to a variety of social and recreational activities, including group outings, sports events, cultural programs, and hobby-based clubs. These activities are chosen to match the interests and abilities of our clients, encouraging fun, friendship, and skill development.
  • Educational and Employment Opportunities: Understanding the importance of personal growth and economic participation, we support our clients in accessing educational courses, vocational training, and employment opportunities. Our goal is to help individuals achieve their learning and career aspirations.
  • Volunteering and Civic Participation: To foster a sense of belonging and contribution, we encourage and support our clients in volunteering and participating in civic activities. This not only benefits the community but also enhances our clients' self-esteem and social networks.
  • Personal Development Programs: Our services include personal development programs designed to build confidence, social skills, and independence. These programs are tailored to the individual goals and needs of our clients, supporting them in becoming more active and engaged community members.
  • Transportation Support: Recognizing that access to transportation is a key enabler of community participation, we provide assistance with transportation arrangements to ensure our clients can attend activities and appointments with ease.

Join Our Community Support Program

Embark on a journey of community engagement and personal growth with Care28. Our Community Support services are here to connect you with the activities, opportunities, and resources that will enrich your life and strengthen your community ties. Contact us today to learn more about how we can support you or your loved one in becoming a more active, engaged, and fulfilled community member.

At Care28, we are dedicated to facilitating access to the NDIS for eligible individuals, offering a spectrum of services tailored to meet the unique needs of each person we serve.
