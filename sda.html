Specialist Disability Accommodation (SDA) Services

At Disability Services 24/7, we understand that finding the right home is more than just a matter of comfort—it's about enabling individuals with disabilities to live independently, securely, and with dignity. Our Specialist Disability Accommodation (SDA) services are designed to meet these needs by offering tailored housing solutions that cater to the unique requirements of each individual.

Our SDA Services: Empowering Independence

SDA services at Disability Services 24/7 are an integral part of our commitment to enhancing the lives of people with significant and permanent disabilities. Our offerings include:

  • Customized Living Solutions: We provide a range of SDA housing options, from apartments and townhouses to single-dwelling units, all designed with accessibility and independence in mind. Each property is equipped with features that support the daily needs and safety of our clients.
  • Personalized Support: Understanding that each individual's needs are unique, our team works closely with clients to identify the most suitable accommodation options that align with their preferences, requirements, and NDIS plans.
  • Community Integration: Our SDA properties are located in friendly and inclusive communities, ensuring clients have access to local amenities, public transport, and social activities. This promotes a sense of belonging and encourages community participation.
  • Quality and Safety Assurance: The safety and well-being of our clients are paramount. All our SDA accommodations meet the highest standards of safety, quality, and compliance with NDIS guidelines, ensuring a secure living environment.
  • Ongoing Assistance and Support: Disability Services 24/7 provides continuous support to our clients, assisting with maintenance issues, modifications, and any other needs that may arise, ensuring a worry-free living experience
